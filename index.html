Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the IELTS preparation course?

Our course is designed to be flexible to accommodate varying schedules. You can study at your own pace, and the duration depends on your individual progress.

Are the study materials included in the course fee?

Yes, the course fee covers access to comprehensive study materials, including interactive lessons, practice tests, and mock exams.

Can I access the course from any location worldwide?

Absolutely! Our online platform allows you to access the IELTS preparation course from anywhere in the world, providing convenience and flexibility.

Our learning plans take into account your strengths and weaknesses. Upon enrollment, you'll undergo an assessment to tailor the course content to meet your specific needs.

Yes, our support team is available to assist you throughout your IELTS preparation journey. Whether you have technical questions or need guidance on course content, we're here to help.
